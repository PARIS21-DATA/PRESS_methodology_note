Measuring and estimating funding to data and statistics

Measuring and estimating funding to data and statistics

Version 2 (Updated on 1 November 2022)

Why this ebook?

PARIS21 produces the Partner Report on Support to Statistics (PRESS) annually to report on trends in support to statistics. The methodology is applied retrospectively for all previous years to ensure comparability over time. This document presents the methodology used in PRESS.
